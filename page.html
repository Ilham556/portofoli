About Me

I am a dedicated Self-Taught Developer with a burning passion for bridging the physical and digital worlds. My journey is fueled by curiosity in Internet of Things (IoT), applying intelligence through Machine Learning, and presenting it all through beautiful Webflow designs.

I don't just write code; I solve real-world problems. From automating aquarium maintenance to predicting machinery faults, I thrive on creating end-to-end solutions that are robust, scalable, and user-centric.
